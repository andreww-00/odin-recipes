Chili

Description

This chili recipe is the perfect winter dinner thats sure to keep you warm and happy through the holiday season. Enjoy this hearty meal with cornbread, cheese, or sour cream and it's sure to bring a smile to everyone's faces.

This beginner recipe is perfect for a learning chef ready to impress their friends. Or an experienced chef might enjoy this simple recipe as a wind down on a busy weeknight

Ingredients

  • one onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • one can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • one can of beans

Recipe

  1. Brown ground beef
  2. Sautee onions and garlic
  3. add crushed tomatoes beef and beans
  4. let simmer for 30 minutes
